Security Measures

At Wealth-Wise, we prioritize the security of your financial data. Below are the key security measures we implement:

All sensitive user data is encrypted using advanced AES-256 encryption, ensuring your information is protected.

🛡 Secure Authentication

We implement multi-factor authentication (MFA) to add an extra layer of security to your account.

We do not share your financial data with third parties without your consent.

⚠ Regular Security Audits

Our platform undergoes routine security checks to identify and fix vulnerabilities.
